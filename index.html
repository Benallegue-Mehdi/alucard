Alucard

Bio

Alucard is the dhampir (half-human, half-vampire) son of Dracula, the antagonist of the Castlevania series, and his mother Lisa.

Lisa created medicine for those who suffered from the epidemic. Due to the witch trials in Europe at the time, Lisa was apprehended and executed. Alucard was present shortly before Lisa's death, but his mother requested that he allowed the mob to kill her, under the belief that it would ultimately save their souls from eternal damnation.

Upon learning about his wife's death, Dracula vowed revenge. In 1476, he began his war upon humanity. However, his mother's death and admonition not to hate humanity caused Alucard to take up arms against his father.

Appearance

Alucard is a man in his late teens with long white blond hair. His most recurring garb is the one from Castlevania: Symphony of the Night. Fitting with that game's setting, Alucard wears 18th century style clothing. He wears a black jacket consisting of side seams held together by four square brooches that run down vertically and a belt. Around his neck he wears a large white cravat. Over the jacket, Alucard wears a long overcoat with a golden decorative pattern. He also wears a long cape, black on the outside and white on the inside. Alucard usually is portrayed with is the Alucard Sword, a family heirloom from his mother.
